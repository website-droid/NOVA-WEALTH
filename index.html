• If you have a tablet or another phone logged into the same Google account, go to Settings → Google → see the email at the top.
• Ask a family member if they ever received an email from you – the "From" address is your email.
• Contact Google Support with the phone's IMEI number (on the box or under battery) – they may help if you prove ownership.
